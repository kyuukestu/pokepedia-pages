Team Aqua

Sociopathic eco-terrorists seeking the total extermination of human life to return the world to the Pokémon.

Eco-Terrorists
High-Level Threat
Status: Fugitive Insurgency
The Great Deluge

Team Aqua is no longer a structured organization—it is a global maritime plague. Driven by a misanthropic interpretation of oceanic expansion, they view every pier, ship, and coastal city as an insult to the primordial sea. Despite their failure to control Kyogre, their ideology has only hardened: if the sea will not rise to swallow humanity, they will tear humanity down into the sea.

Total Human Erasure

The belief that humanity is a terminal parasite. Expanding the seas is not just about habitat; it is about the cleansing of civilization.

Maritime Insurgency

Hiding in unmapped deep-sea trenches and raiding cargo ships, ferries, and oil rigs for resources to fuel their crusade.

Deep-Sea Guerrilla Tactics

Specializing in ambush attacks using Sharpedo-led boarding parties and high-pressure underwater explosives.

Abyssal Raiders

Since the collapse of their Lilycove headquarters, Team Aqua has retreated to Seafloor Caverns and mobile sub-aquatic bases. They prowl international waters, raiding transportation routes for food, fuel, and medical supplies. They do not negotiate; they scavenge.

DANGER: ACTIVE PIRACY ZONE

Archie (The Radical)

A charismatic sociopath whose love for Pokémon is matched only by his hatred for human infrastructure. He leads from the shadows of the abyss.

The Shattered Admins

Shelly and Matt coordinate decentralized cells across the world’s oceans, maintaining a network of hidden Seafloor Caverns.

Encounter Protocols

Aqua's attacks are surgical—disabling ship engines and using Water-type Pokémon to drag crews into the depths. Merchant Vessels and Trainers are urged to exercise caution during deep-sea operations.

Travel Warning
All maritime transport between Hoenn and Sinnoh must now be escorted by League-sanctioned security. Unescorted vessels are considered "prey" by Aqua raiding parties.
