Team Dim Sun

The defunct syndicate that sought to plunge Almia into eternal subservience through hypnotic technology.

Defunct Syndicate
Wave-Tech Specialists
Origins: Altru Inc.
The Hypnotic Hegemony

Team Dim Sun was a highly organized paramilitary group that operated as the clandestine arm of Altru Inc. Their philosophy was simple: absolute control. By utilizing specialized radio waves to "dim" the spirits of Pokémon, they bypassed the need for companionship or Poké Balls, turning the wildlife of Almia into an automated drone army.

Though twice-defeated, the remnants of Dim Sun lurk in the shadows, rebranding themselves as Team Debonair.

Gigaremo Units

Massive stationary machines that emit hypnotic waves, overriding a Pokémon's willpower and forcing them into labor or combat.

Miniremo Portables

Handheld versions of the Gigaremo technology, allowing Grunts to control individual Pokémon without the need for Poké Balls.

Wave Inversion

The science of "dimming" a Pokémon's spirit. Unlike Cipher’s Shadow tech, this is purely vibrational and temporary, yet physically taxing.

The Incredible Machine

The ultimate evolution of Dim Sun tech: Altru Tower converted into a region-wide hypnotic transmitter.

The Luminous Crystal

The antithesis of Dim Sun’s dark ambitions is the Luminous Crystal. Originally corrupted into the Shadow Crystal to power the Incredible Machine, it was purified by the three Tears of the Princes.

Currently, the Crystal is safely housed within the Reformed Altru Inc. headquarters. It serves as a massive purification beacon, emitting a "Capture Wave" that protects the region from residual dark energy and prevents the reactivation of any remaining Gigaremo units.

Status: STABLE & PURIFIED
High Command

Dim Sun’s strength lay in its corporate backing. With access to Altru’s bottomless R&D budget, they developed technology that even the Ranger Union struggled to counter until the discovery of the Vorton and the Luminous Crystal.

Blake Hall (The President)

The mastermind who used Altru Inc. as a financial shell to fund Dim Sun’s global conquest. Vanished without a trace after an encounter with Darkrai.

The Sinis Trio

Elite commanders Ice, Lavana, and Heath. Known for high-level tactical strikes and utilizing "extra-large" controlled Pokémon.

Mr. Kincaid

The Vice President of Dim Sun who operated undercover as a teacher in the Ranger Academy.

Ranger Intelligence
While Team Dim Sun is officially disbanded, rogue "Miniremo" units still circulate in the Black Market. In RP, these units can be found in the possession of poachers, requiring Rangers to use Target Clear to neutralize the machines before the Pokémon can be safely captured or assisted.
SAFETY ADVISORY: Interaction with unshielded Gigaremo waves can cause nausea and temporary disorientation in humans. Do not attempt to dismantle a unit without a certified Capture Styler or protective gear.
