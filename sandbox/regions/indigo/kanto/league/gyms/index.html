League Requirements
Badge Collection

Trainers must collect all 8 official Indigo badges. Secondary gym badges do not count toward Conference qualification.

Dojo Tradition

Unofficial gyms often follow traditional "Dojo" rules, such as restricted item use or specific move bans.

Regional Recognition

While not providing badges, defeating local masters is often a requirement for specific "G-Men" scouting programs.
