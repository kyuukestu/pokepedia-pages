Indigo Region

Dual-Region Zone

While geographically distinct, Kanto and Johto share the Indigo Plateau League and the Indigo Conference authority — forming a single administrative zone unique in the Pokémon world.

Inter-Regional Travel

Travel between regions is available through a variety of means. Most trainers rely on their Pokémon once they've obtained the relevant permit — but commercial options are available to everyone.

Cruise Lines
S.S. Anne, S.S. Tidal and regional services.
Cross-Regional Rail
Magnet Train, Kalos Rail & more.
Regional Airlines
Scheduled civilian aviation services.
Flying Mount
Requires a valid Aviation Permit.
Ground Mount
Requires a valid Ground Mount Permit.
Dive Mount
Inter-island routes only.
Permit Requirements
Flying and ground mount travel requires a valid permit issued by the Pokémon League Aviation Authority (PLAA) or Pokémon League Ground Authority (PLGA) respectively. Commercial transport has no permit requirements for passengers.
