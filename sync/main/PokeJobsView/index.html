Below is the pay table for jobs based on a trainer's rank and job difficulty. Ranks are Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum, with job difficulties rated from 0 to 5 stars.

Basic Jobs of that rank; typically involves simple tasks that pose minimal danger but may require Pokémon labor: e.g., manual labor, running deliveries, etc.
A Job of Moderate Difficulty. May involve combat or finesse. Accomplishable by a single trainer. e.g., Manufacturing, Crafting, etc. At least one Gym Badge (or its equivalent) required.
Jobs of ardent difficulty, a team of trainers is recommended. At least three Gym Badges (or their equivalent) required.
The difficulty of these jobs is expected to exceed the rank they’re assigned to, even with a team you can expect these to be grueling tasks. May require several days to complete. At least five Gym Badges (or their equivalent) required.
Even with a team of trainers, death looms for the careless trainer. These jobs may also demand time commitments measured in weeks or more. At least five Gym Badges (or their equivalent) required.
Make no mistake, you are going to die. Payment will be made to your next of kin. e.g., Ultra Wars.
