What is Fortitude?

Fortitude is the fundamental energy reservoir that flows between trainers and their Pokémon. This mystical force serves as the bridge connecting human will to Pokémon power, enabling the Synchronization Bond and fueling a Pokémon's abilities to an extend hitherto unseen.

The average trainer begins their journey at Bronze I with approx.

50 points
of Fortitude, a modest wellspring that grows stronger with experience, dedication, and the bonds forged with their partners.
